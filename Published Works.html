Background of the Pseudonym:

The pseudonym "Augustine de Valle" is used by Brianne Gabrielle C. Rodriguez, a Literary Writer. The origin of the name "Augustine" came from the song "August" by Taylor Swift as the character in the song is named Augustine. Brianne was fond of the name "Augustine" therefore she took inspiration by using the name as a writer. The pseudonym "de Valle" came from the writer's favorite flowers which are Lilies of the Valley. The flower symbolizes purity of the heart and soul.
